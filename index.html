Our Story

We are Sam and Louise, passionate caterers who launched our products at the Clevedon Farmers Market. We started producing Parsley Mayo and Tasty Tom Sauce to accompany our Bacon and Egg baps at the market.

A decade on, we still have an overwhelmingly positive response from our market goers and local community. Our Parsley Mayo is produced in small batches from our Clevedon kitchen, using locally grown parsley from Clevedon Herbs and Produce.

We feel now is the right time to expand into a wider network of specialty food stores so even more people can enjoy our products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted condiments.

Every batch is handcrafted in our Clevedon kitchen using locally sourced ingredients. Our parsley comes from Clevedon Herbs and Produce, and we use free range eggs in our mayo. No mass production here, just genuine small-batch quality you can taste.
Our Parsley Mayo is incredibly versatile. It is perfect with bacon and eggs, fish, chicken, in sandwiches, through potato salad, as a dip, or on grazing platters. Basically, anywhere you would use regular mayo but want something with more flavour and personality.
Tasty Tom is a brilliant BBQ accompaniment, pairs wonderfully with cheese, works as a pizza sauce, and adds real depth to casseroles and soups. It is one of those products that once you try it, you will find a use for it with almost every meal.
Our products are made with simple, whole ingredients. Please check the ingredients list on each product for any specific dietary concerns. If you have questions about allergens, feel free to contact us at clevedon.cuisine@gmail.com and we are happy to help.
Because our products are made with real, fresh ingredients without preservatives, we recommend refrigerating after opening and using within a few weeks. Check the label on your jar for the specific best-before date.
You can find us at the Clevedon Farmers Market every Sunday morning and at select stores in Clevedon Village. We are currently expanding into specialty food stores across New Zealand. Keep an eye on our website and Instagram for updates on new stockist locations.
We are working on making our products available for online ordering and delivery. In the meantime, the best way to get your hands on Clevedon Cuisine is to visit us at the Clevedon Farmers Market or pop into one of our local stockists. Follow us on Instagram for the latest updates.
